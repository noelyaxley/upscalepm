# UpScale PM → Club Development Advisory: Repositioning Plan

**Date:** 7 March 2026
**Goal:** Reposition upscalepm.com.au from generic client-side PM to **Australia's specialist independent advisor for NSW club redevelopments**.

---

## Executive Summary

The NSW registered clubs industry has ~1,200-1,400 clubs generating $7B annually, with an estimated 170-275 redevelopment projects per year worth $2-4B. **Almost no PM firm positions exclusively as a club redevelopment specialist.** Cerno Management is the closest competitor but has no public web presence. This is a massive positioning gap.

UpScale's existing Granville Diggers project becomes the anchor case study — proof of sector expertise that launches the new positioning.

---

## Part 1: Brand & Messaging Repositioning

### New Brand Line
**UpScale Project Management | Club Development Advisory**

### New Tagline
"Independent Advisors for Club & Golf Club Redevelopments"

### Core Message
"We help club boards and CEOs successfully deliver major capital projects — from feasibility to opening night."

### Positioning Angle
**Strategic advisor to club boards** — NOT a builder, NOT a generic PM.
The key differentiator is **independence**: architects and builders have competing incentives. UpScale protects the club's interests.

### Voice & Tone
- Authoritative but approachable
- Governance-aware (boards, fiduciary duty, member expectations)
- Hospitality-focused language (venues, experiences, members) NOT construction-focused (hard hats, sites)
- Club-industry insider, not outsider looking in
