**Market reality**: This is a micro-niche. There are perhaps 50-80 clubs in active planning or construction at any given time across NSW. Most will never search Google — they will rely on industry referrals, ClubsNSW contacts, or existing relationships. Google Ads captures the 10-20% who do search, plus the broader "construction PM" market that includes club decision-makers researching adjacent topics.

## 4. Bidding Strategy

### Per-Campaign Bidding Approach

| Campaign | Phase 1 (Mo 1-3) | Phase 2 (Mo 4-6) | Phase 3 (Mo 7-12) |
|----------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| C1: Club Redevelopment | Maximize Clicks (Max CPC: $15) | Maximize Conversions | Target CPA ($150) |
| C2: Construction PM | Maximize Clicks (Max CPC: $18) | Maximize Conversions | Target CPA ($250) |
| C3: Free Report | Maximize Clicks (Max CPC: $8) | Maximize Conversions | Target CPA ($40) |
| C4: Remarketing | Maximize Clicks (Max CPC: $3) | Maximize Conversions | Target CPA ($80) |

### Transition Triggers

Follow the standard decision tree from the bidding reference:

1. **Maximize Clicks → Maximize Conversions**: When campaign achieves 15+ conversions in 30 days
2. **Maximize Conversions → Target CPA**: When CPA standard deviation < 20% over 14 days AND 30+ conversions
3. **Portfolio strategy**: If C1 and C2 individually have < 15 conversions/month but combined have 30+, create a portfolio bid strategy across both campaigns

### Max CPC Safeguards

Set Max CPC caps during Maximize Clicks phase to prevent wasteful high-CPC clicks:

| Campaign | Max CPC Cap | Rationale |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| C1: Club Redevelopment | $15 | Target CPA ~$150 / estimated 10% CVR / 1.5 safety = $10 theoretical, rounded up |
| C2: Construction PM | $18 | Higher CPC niche, competitive keywords |
| C3: Free Report | $8 | Lower-intent traffic, high volume needed |
| C4: Remarketing | $3 | Display/remarketing CPCs are low |

### Portfolio Bid Strategy (Month 3+)

If individual campaigns lack sufficient conversion volume for Smart Bidding:
- Create a **Portfolio Bid Strategy** combining C1 + C2 (both target consultations)
- Set shared Target CPA at 1.1-1.2x the historical blended CPA
- Minimum 3 campaigns recommended; consider adding a brand campaign if competitor brand bidding emerges

### Key Bidding Rules

- Never lower Target CPA by more than 15% at once
- Adjust targets max every 14 days
- If CPA increases >15% after budget increase, roll back to previous budget
- Use the **3x Kill Rule**: If any ad group spends 3x target CPA with 0 conversions, pause and review

3. **Digital content moat**: With 35+ published insights articles (many specifically about club redevelopments — Dee Why RSL, Penrith RSL, Canterbury Leagues, etc.), UpScale has significantly more topical content than any competitor. This is a major SEO advantage and gives Google Ads campaigns strong Quality Scores via landing page relevance.

4. **Board-level governance reporting**: Most PM firms talk about "project delivery." UpScale can own the "governance" angle — structured monthly board reports, committee briefings, member communication support. This resonates with club boards who need to justify spending to members.

5. **Free planning report as a lead magnet**: No competitor offers a free property development report. This gives UpScale a unique top-of-funnel asset that no competitor can match without building the infrastructure.

6. **Club-specific landing page**: The /landing/clubs page is purpose-built for club boards. Most competitors have generic service pages. A dedicated, conversion-optimized landing page with club-specific pain points, FAQs, and case studies will outperform generic competitor websites in ad Quality Score and conversion rate.

7. **Small firm advantage**: Club boards (5-20 people making decisions) may prefer dealing with a named individual (Noel) over a large faceless firm. Lean into the founder-led positioning in all ad copy.

- **Barrington Group**: Likely running some Google Ads (they have a digital presence) but focused on general club consulting, not specifically redevelopment PM
- **RCP / Gallagher Jeffs / FTA**: Likely running broader construction PM ads that may occasionally show for club-adjacent terms

- **Club Web Design**: One firm (clubwebdesign.com.au) specifically offers Google Ads management to clubs — but this is a vendor to clubs, not a competitor for advisory services

**Bottom line**: UpScale likely has an open field for club-specific Google Ads keywords. The competition is for broader construction PM terms.
