## Tips

- Filter: Photos only, Orientation: Horizontal/Landscape
- Avoid: hard hats, hi-vis vests (except service-construction.jpg), generic stock poses
- Prefer: Australian-looking settings, natural materials, warm lighting
- Avoid: American-style interiors (too corporate), European (too formal)
- Gaming floor: search "casino interior" not "pokies" — more results
- Club interiors: search "members club interior" or "private club lounge" for upscale feel
